# Kekova Sea Kayaking & Snorkeling Adventure

Join an unforgettable sea kayaking journey through the breathtaking Kekova region, where ancient history and unspoiled nature blend into one spectacular adventure! Paddle over sunken ruins, swim in various secluded bays, spot wildlife, and paddle by a cave on this full-day experience designed for nature lovers and adventure seekers. This tour is aimed at intermediate to advanced paddlers or kayak enthusiats with good physical condition for longer paddling.

## Tour Itinerary

## Meeting Point: Kaş King’s Tomb – 07:30 AM / Üçağız: 08:15

* Meet your expert guide and fellow adventurers.
* If you’re staying on the Kaş Peninsula, pick-up can be arranged.
* Kalkan transfer available for an additional €10.

## Journey to Üçağız

* Enjoy a scenic drive through rugged village roads, passing Mediterranean landscapes.
* Stop at a local gas station to pick up water or last-minute supplies.
* Arrive at Üçağız, a charming fishing village and the gateway to Kekova.
* For those who were not able to have an early breakfast, you can grab some Turkish pastries from the local bakery.

## Sea Kayaking Adventure Begins

* Get equipped with sea kayaks, life jackets, dry bags and paddles.
* Your guide will provide snorkeling gear, but bringing your own is recommended.
* Receive a detailed paddling and safety briefing before the start of the kayaking tour.

## Paddle Over the Sunken City of Kekova

* Begin your kayaking journey, paddling directly to the Sunken City of Kekova.
* Glide over ancient Lycian ruins, submerged beneath crystal-clear waters after an earthquake centuries ago.
* Observe stone staircases, walls, and harbor remains, all visible beneath the surface.
* Since this area is protected, swimming is not allowed, but you’ll have the closest view of the ruins from your kayak.

## Swim & Snorkel in Hidden Bays

* Take refreshing swim breaks in Kekova’s hidden bays, surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty and ruins.
* Your guide will provide snorkeling gear, allowing you to explore underwater ruins and marine life up close.
* Keep an eye out for wild goats, often spotted on the surrounding cliffs and islands.

## Picnic Lunch with a View

* Take a break on the shoreline and enjoy a picnic-style lunch featuring a fresh sandwich, fruits, and local snacks.
* Relax, soak up the sun, and enjoy the tranquil Mediterranean scenery.

## Paddling by a cave & Spot Wildlife

* Paddle past a mysterious sea cave, once home to Mediterranean monk seals.
* If you’re lucky, you may spot loggerhead sea turtles gliding gracefully through the waters!

## Follow the Andriake Stream – Turtle Spotting

* Continue your journey along the Andriake Stream, a protected nesting area for sea turtles.
* Observe these majestic creatures in their natural habitat, a true highlight of the tour.

## Final Paddle and Wrap Up

* Take in the stunning coastal views and enjoy one last swim stop before finishing the journey.
* Upon arrival, you’ll have time to shower at a near-by camp site and change into your dry clothes and enjoy a refreshing drink if you like.

## Return to Kaş

* Hop back into the van for the 40-minute scenic drive back to Kaş.
* Expected arrival in Kaş: Around 16:00 – 16:30 PM.

## Why Choose This Tour?

* Explore a sunken Lycian city – Paddle over centuries-old underwater ruins.
* Snorkeling & swimming – Enjoy refreshing breaks in secluded Mediterranean bays.
* Wildlife encounters – Spot sea turtles, wild goats, and marine life.
* Paddle by a cave – Once home to the rare Mediterranean monk seal.
* Perfect mix of adventure & relaxation – Paddle, snorkel, and soak in stunning views.
* Small group experience – Personalized tour with expert guides.
* More paddling time on the water with kayakers with more experience.

Book now and paddle into history on this once-in-a-lifetime adventure!

**Paddle distance:** 17 km

# Kekova & Aperlai Full-Day Sea Kayaking Adventure

Embark on an extraordinary sea kayaking adventure, exploring the sunken cities of Kekova and Aperlai! This full-day journey is designed for intermediate to advanced paddlers, offering a perfect blend of adventure, history, and breathtaking natural beauty. Paddle through crystal-clear waters, snorkel above ancient ruins in Aperlai, and hike along the legendary Lycian Way.

## Tour Itinerary

## Meeting Point: Kaş King’s Tomb – 07:30 AM

* Meet your expert guide and fellow adventurers.
* Pick-up available for those staying on the Kaş Peninsula.
* Kalkan transfer available for an additional €10.

## Journey to Üçağız

* Enjoy a scenic drive along rugged village roads and stunning Mediterranean landscapes.
* Stop at a local gas station to grab water and any last-minute supplies.
* Arrive at Üçağız, a charming fishing village and the gateway to Kekova.
* For those who were not able to have an early breakfast, you can grab some Turkish pastries from the local bakery across from the gas station.

## Sea Kayaking Adventure Begins

* Get equipped with sea kayaks, life jackets, dry bags and paddles.
* Your guide will provide snorkeling gear, but bringing your own is recommended.
* Receive a detailed paddling and safety briefing before the start of the kayaking tour.

## Paddle to Simena & the Sunken City of Kekova

* Begin your kayaking journey toward Simena, the iconic castle village.
* Stop by the most famous Lycian tomb, submerged in the water, with the dramatic backdrop of Simena Castle.
* Glide over Kekova’s legendary sunken city, where centuries-old staircases, stone walls, and harbor structures rest beneath the surface.
* Learn about the history of Kekova, once a bustling Lycian trading port, now a mystical underwater archaeological site.
* After stopping for a short swim break at Tersane (Shipyard) Bay continue paddling along the stunning coastline toward Aperlai, a lesser-known but incredible ancient city.

## Lunch at a Fisherman’s House

* After an exhilarating morning on the water, enjoy a delicious home-cooked meal at a local fisherman’s house.
* Feast on grilled fish, accompanied by the best fries in the region and fresh Mediterranean salad.

## Short Hike Along the Lycian Way

* Leave your kayaks behind for a scenic hike along a section of the Lycian Way, one of Turkey’s most famous long-distance trails.
* Walk among ancient ruins, offering a different perspective of Aperlai’s historical significance.
* Enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of the coastline and the Mediterranean Sea from the top of the city walls.

## Discover Aperlai – The Hidden Ancient City

* Snorkel above ruins hidden beneath the sea, including old harbors, sunken structures, and remnants of daily life from thousands of years ago.
* Uncover the fascinating history of royal purple dye-making, a once-luxurious industry that symbolized power and wealth in the ancient world.

## Experience the Thrill of Kayak Sailing

* If conditions allow, hoist small sails onto your kayaks and let the wind guide you back—a unique and thrilling experience that adds an extra element of adventure to your journey.

## Return to Üçağız & Wrap-Up

* Arrive back at Üçağız, where you’ll have 15-20 minutes of free time while the guides and team clean the kayaks and equipment.
* Take a leisurely stroll through the village, grab a refreshing drink, or simply relax by the water.

## Return to Kaş

* Hop back into the van for a 40-minute scenic drive back to Kaş.
* Estimated arrival time in Kaş: 17:00 – 17:30 PM.

## Why Choose This Tour?

* Paddle through two legendary sunken cities – Kekova & Aperlai.
* Small group experience with professional guides.
* Snorkeling above ancient ruins in Tersane Bay and the sunken ruins of Aperlai.
* Hike a short sectionpf the famous Lycian Way.
* Kayak sailing option for a thrilling, wind-powered experience.
* Authentic local lunch at a fisherman’s house.
* More paddling time on the water with kayakers with more experience.

Book now and experience the adventure of a lifetime!

**Paddle distance:** 15 km
**Hiking:** 1.5 km

# Classic Sea Kayak Tour Details

## Tour Itinerary

## Meeting Point: Kaş King’s Tomb – 07:30 AM

* Meet your expert guide and fellow adventurers.
* If you’re staying on the Kaş Peninsula, pick-up can be arranged.

## Journey to Üçağız

* Enjoy a scenic drive along the rugged village roads and Mediterranean landscape.
* There will be a stop at a gas station to pick up water or any last-minute supplies.
* For those who were not able to have an early breakfast, you can grab some Turkish pastries from the local bakery across from the gas station.
* Arrive at Üçağız, a charming fishing village and the gateway to Kekova.

## Sea Kayaking Adventure Begins

* Get equipped with sea kayaks, life jackets, dry bags and paddles.
* Your guide will offer snorkeling gear, but bringing your own is recommended.
* Receive a detailed paddling and safety briefing before the start of the kayaking tour.

## Paddle to Tersane Bay

* Glide past small islands with Roman and Lycian ruins and the stunning Mediterranean coastline.
* Reach Tersane Bay, home to ruins of an ancient Lycian shipyard and more.
* Take a short break to stretch, enjoy the peaceful surroundings, and refuel with snacks provided by your guide.

## Explore the Sunken City of Kekova

* Paddle single file along the edge of the famous Sunken City.
* Before reaching the ruins, your guide will share a brief history about this ancient settlement, submerged by an earthquake centuries ago.
* Observe the half-submerged walls, staircases, and ancient harbor beneath the crystal-clear water.
* Since swimming is not allowed in this protected area, you’ll explore from your kayak as you glide over the ruins.

## Visit Simena – The Castle Village

* Cross the bay to Simena (Kaleköy), a charming village with no road access. The best way to reach it is by boat or kayak.
* Marvel at the old castle perched above the village, offering breathtaking panoramic views.

## Lunch with a Stunning View

* Enjoy a delicious meal at a local waterfront restaurant, with your choice of: grilled fish, traditional meatballs (köfte), grilled chicken, or a vegetarian/vegan option.
* Accompanied by home-made fries and a fresh salad.

## Free Time in Simena

* Explore the castle ruins for breathtaking views of Kekova and the surrounding islands.
* Wander through quaint stone streets, lined with flower-covered houses and handmade souvenirs.
* Try the famous homemade ice cream unique to Simena.
* Relax by the waterfront or go for a refreshing swim in the crystal-clear water.

## Return Paddle to Üçağız

* Explore shallow waters close to the Castle Village and glide past ancient Lycian tombs hidden along the rocks on the return to the starting point.

## Wrap-Up in Üçağız

* After arriving back in Üçağız, you’ll have 15-20 minutes of free time while the guides wash the kayaks and equipment.
* Take a leisurely stroll, grab a refreshing drink or soak in the peaceful village atmosphere.

## Return to Kaş

* Hop back in the van for a 40-minute drive back to Kaş.
