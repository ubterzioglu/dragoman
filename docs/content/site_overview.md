# Dragoman Turkey — Public Site Overview

Source domain: `https://dragoman-turkey.com/`

The public website is a WordPress/WooCommerce site for Dragoman Diving and Outdoors in Kaş, Antalya. It has English, Turkish and French language sections.

## Reusable content groups

1. Company / About
2. Diving services and SSI courses
3. Sea kayak daily tours and multi-day tours
4. Outdoor activities: hiking, biking, coasteering, multi-activity packages
5. Transportation and accommodation
6. Lifeguard / cankurtarma center
7. Shop products
8. Blog posts and travel/tour articles
9. Contact and reservation information
10. Legal pages: privacy policy, terms, return/cancellation policy

## Known technical notes

- The site appears to be built with WordPress and WooCommerce.
- Some legacy text contains encoding artifacts. Cleanup is recommended before reuse.
- Public content can be crawled, but private WordPress source/PHP/database files cannot be downloaded from the public website.
